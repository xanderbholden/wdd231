Petapa Commerce

Overview

Purpose

This website will serve as a resource for the city of San Miguel Petapa to create a Chamber of Commerce with the region of San Miguel Petapa, Guatemala, Guatemala; Expose the business for the community of this part of Guatemala, Get a Network of business relationships. It aims to promote local economic growth, support local business, and foster a sense of community between the business.

Target Market

Business in and around San Miguel Petapa, include Business Owners and Patrons.

Site Goals

  • Improve member engagement by providing valuable resources and networking opportunities
  • Attract new investor to San Miguel Petapa and expand the job opportunities
  • Colaborate with Government and high companies to provide business and economic to San Miguel Petapa
